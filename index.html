Core Problem: Unaffordable Housing in Toronto

Key Issue: Skyrocketing house prices make it difficult for many families to afford homes.

Why Public Housing is a Viable Solution

Addressing High Demand and Limited Space: Toronto's high population density and limited land availability make traditional housing models challenging. Public housing provides a scalable and sustainable solution.

Conclusion

Choosing public housing in Toronto is a practical solution for those struggling with the high cost of living, providing affordable, stable accommodation, and complementing the city's plans to improve transportation.
